NewsTalk AI - An Innovative platform designed to revolutionize information consumption through hyper-personalization.By moving away from generic reporting, the system utilises artificial intelligence to adapt news delivery based on a user’s age, profession, and available time. Key features include dynamic summarisation, profession-specific interpretations, and multilingual audio generation with age-appropriate narration styles.

Project Vision - To build a platform that gives people the oppertunity to efficiently customize information based on their available time, profession and age. The platform focuses on providing users with information they need to know in essential in the most interesting and optimised way by making use of AI.

Core Features include:
    Personalized Feed
    AI Summaries
    Audio Briefings
    Visual Explainers
    Parent Mode
    Family Profiles
    Multilingual Support
